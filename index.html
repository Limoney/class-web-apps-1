Fifth Norwegian chess tournament
Carlsen participated in the fifth Norwegian chess tournament from June 6 to 16. He was underwhelming, with a performance rating of 2755, the lowest since 2015 (2670 in the European Team Chess Championship). In the end, he finished ninth in the round robin with 4/9 (+1−2=6), losing to Aronian and Kramnik and winning Karjakin. Aronian won the game 6/9 (+3−0=6).
